## Objective

"Remove the M4 rig's greedy-only decode limitation by wiring SGLang per-request sampling params into the MLX backend's token-selection sites using mlx-lm 0.31.2's make_sampler. This is the sampling-side blocker on thinking mode — the fleet's bake-off leaders are thinking ships (qwen36-dense 62.3% opencode on the 3090), and the M4's Qwen3.x presets currently infinite-loop in <think> under greedy decode, forcing thinking to be proxied off entirely for agentic work."


## Hypothesis

"Routing SGLang per-request sampling params (temp/top-p/top-k/min-p) into the MLX backend via mlx-lm 0.31.2's make_sampler (i) terminates the Qwen3.x infinite-<think> loop class — probe_thinking.py reaches </think> and returns VERIFIED on qwen36 AND on a preset whose baseline first CONFIRMED the loop (Qwen3.5-27B-4bit if it fits RAM, else qwen35-9b-8bit as a labeled RAM proxy) under model-recommended sampling — while (ii) keeping greedy-path decode tok/s within ±3% (server-log gen throughput) and greedy outputs token-identical. Falsified on (i) if the loop persists under sampling on a preset that demonstrably looped at baseline (which would confirm the README-line-206 checkpoint/DeltaNet-quantization hypothesis instead); a preset that does NOT loop at baseline is untestable for (i) and is dropped, not counted as a pass. Falsified on (ii) if overhead exceeds 3% on the greedy path."

## Success criteria

- Greedy identity: temperature=0 (top_k==1) requests produce token-identical completions pre/post patch on the fixed 3-prompt set (receipt: saved completions in benchmarks/sampling-ab-<date>.md).
- Sampling live: temp=1.0 5-repeat run yields >=4/5 distinct completions; unit sanity script passes (greedy rows == argmax, sampled tokens only from unfiltered set).
- probe_thinking.py returns exit 0 (THINKING VERIFIED) on qwen36 with model-recommended sampling, AND on a preset whose BASELINE established the loop (Qwen3.5-27B-4bit looper, or labeled qwen35-9b-8bit proxy) the known-loop case reaches </think> with finish_reason=stop within max_tokens — OR the documented null (kill criterion 3) with transcripts. A preset that returned VERIFIED at baseline is recorded and dropped from this criterion, not counted as a win.
- Perf: greedy-path decode tok/s within ±3% of baseline and sampled-path cost quantified, both from server-log gen throughput at MR=1 (receipts: log excerpts + bench_quick.sh rows in benchmarks.log).
- Batched correctness: MR=4 mixed-params batch — greedy rows reproducible across reruns, sampled rows vary, no cross-row token bleed (distinct prompts stay on-topic).
- Modality gate: probe_vision.py + probe_codegen.py outcomes unchanged from their current README-matrix status on the tested preset.
- patches/021-mlx-sampling.patch applies clean (git apply --check) on pristine v0.5.12; setup.sh apply glob widened to [0-9][0-9][0-9]-*.patch and a fresh setup.sh run prints '✓ 021-mlx-sampling.patch'; patches/README.md + README.md updated including the corrected site count and no reintroduced v0.5.11 string.


## Kill criteria

- Greedy regression unfixable: if post-patch top_k==1 requests are not token-identical to baseline after one debugging pass, revert the offending sites and record which site diverged (likely a logprob-normalization or lazy-eval-graph change leaking into the greedy branch).
- If heterogeneous per-row sampling in decode_batch_start cannot be made correct without breaking the mx.async_eval lazy contract (tp_worker.py:483), ship homogeneous-batch + MR=1 sampling only (covers the single-user mission) and record the batched limitation in patches/README.md — do not hold the patch hostage to the batched edge case.
- If the infinite-<think> loop persists under model-recommended sampling on a preset whose BASELINE established the loop (the receipt-backed Qwen3.5-27B-4bit looper, or the labeled qwen35-9b-8bit proxy if it looped at baseline), STOP probing: record the null (sampling is not the loop's root cause; strengthens the README-line-206 DeltaNet/router-quant hypothesis feeding queue item 5), keep the sampling patch anyway (it is fleet-parity infrastructure regardless), and update Known Issues accordingly.
- If sampled-path overhead exceeds 25% decode tok/s at MR=1 after trying the batched-sampler and precomputed-sampler-closure variants, land greedy-default + document the cost; do not micro-optimize past 1 day.

## Risks

- mx.compile decorators on apply_top_p/min_p/top_k capture mx.random state; per-shape recompiles on varying batch sizes could add first-call latency spikes — measure, don't assume.

- Loop-repro preset selection risk: qwen35-9b-8bit may not actually loop (8-bit precision; README:71 shows it engaging tool calls), making it a weak disambiguator; the baseline DEGRADED precondition + preferring the INT4 Qwen3.5-27B-4bit looper mitigates counting a non-loop as a fixed loop.
- Patch 021 edits upstream-origin lines (7 of 10 sites), increasing future rebase conflict surface — record the site map in patches/README.md for the next version-rebase gate.
- Sampled thinking traces are longer and non-deterministic: probe max_tokens=2000 may truncate on 27B-class verbosity (README note); bump per-run max_tokens rather than declaring DEGRADED on length alone.
- mlx-lm is unpinned in setup.sh extras — a venv rebuild could land a newer mlx-lm with a changed sample_utils surface; the patch should import defensively and the receipt should record the installed version.
